Welcome to NationHub, a platform developed by flAks , a dedicated developer with a passion for promoting global awareness and understanding. Our platform offers a unique opportunity to explore countries from around the world, including their capitals, languages, populations, and more. By providing interesting and informative information about each country, our goal is to broaden your knowledge and understanding of the world.

At NationHub, our mission is to create a user-friendly and engaging experience that allows you to dive into the diverse cultures and characteristics of countries across the globe. We believe that learning about different nations should be accessible and enjoyable for everyone.

To gather and present country data, NationHub relies on the following APIs:

  • Rest Countries API - Provides comprehensive information about countries, including their names, capitals, languages, populations, and more.
  • API Ninjas - Offers additional data such as GDP, GDP per capita, and currency exchange rates for countries.
  • GeoNames API - Enables the retrieval of city data for each country, including city names and populations.

Through NationHub, flAks aspires to create a platform that fosters curiosity and appreciation for the countries of the world, ultimately contributing to a more interconnected global community.
